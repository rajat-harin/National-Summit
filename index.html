About The Event


Smart city solutions is a 2 day National Summit commencing on 8th March at G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering under the elite banners of IEEE HKN Mu-Pi Chapter and IEEE Nagpur Subsection. The Summit is organized keeping in mind the utmost need of awareness and acquaintance with the concepts of Smart City. We aim at incorporating these concepts and ideas with increased awareness among budding engineers, professionals, Industrialists, fresh and upcoming entrepreneurs. The National Summit will be a perfect epitome of enhancing the quality of living for its citizens through smart technology.
The Summit would tap a range of approaches to “Smart Solutions to Smart Cities” and promote cities that would provide a clean and sustainable environment.
The Summit brings to you a plethora of opportunities to witness Panel Discussions on Smart Communication, Gray Water Sanitation, Green Energy, E-Mobility, E-Governance & Environment Smart Infrastructure and Start-up Showcases.
The following are the main motives of discussion Sessions:

  1. Smart Cities Overview
  2. Green Energy-1
  3. E-Governance
  4. Smart Infrastructure and compact cities
  5. Sustainable transportation
  6. Smart Communication
  7. Environment and Gray Water Sanitation


IEEE is the world’s largest technical- professional society which continuously promotes the development and application of electro technology and allied sciences for the benefit of humanity and for the advancement of the profession.
With the 4,30,000 members spanning across 165 countries of the globe, IEEE fosters the development of standards, that often become national and international standards and produces over 30% of the world’s literature in its related fields, publishing well over 100 peer - reviewed journals.


Eta Kappa Nu (ΗΚΝ) or IEEE-HKN is the international honour society of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE).
G.H Raisoni College of Engineering Nagpur (GHRCE) successfully installed IEEE HKN Mu-Pi Chapter on 25thApril 2018 by the hands of Dr. Ramakrishna Kappagantu, IEEE Region-10 Director (Asia Pacific). The HKN Chapter is running in its third year in India. Mu-Pi chapter at GHRCE facilitates students of Electrical, ETC department to gain insight and knowledge in the field of technology and to network with technical experts at Societies, through workshops, section activities and local chapters, sessions and lectures by technical professionals on upcoming technologies to instill students with the required skill and knowledge to excel in the tech world.


IEEE Nagpur subsection is 2nd subsection established under IEEE Bombay section in September, 2017.
The subsection aims at forming better networking between professional members and student members within Nagpur district of Maharashtra state.
It is also instrumental in boosting Engineering students, professionals and their respective careers. Along with the former, it prepares them for the competent world out there and to accept the globally posed challenges confidently , via various events that we organize to update knowledge and enhance their skill sets.
